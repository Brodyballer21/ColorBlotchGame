What is Roll Your Color?

Roll Your Color is a web-based color grid generator that combines the excitement of gacha mechanics with pixel art creativity. Each roll generates a randomized color pattern that you can collect, save, and show off.

Game Features

  • Multiple Rarity Tiers: From Common to Scenic Rare, each tier offers unique patterns and rewards
  • 8 Secret Rare Designs: Unlock special pixel art characters including Clown, Apex, Portal, and more
  • 8 Scenic Rare Collections: Collect 4-piece panoramic artworks that combine into stunning 80√ó50 images
  • Lucky Number System: Every 30 rolls, get a guaranteed high-tier pull
  • Color Coins Economy: Earn coins to purchase luck boosts and rare boosts
  • Cloud Save: Sign in with Google to save your collection across devices
  • Storage Management: Organize, favorite, and delete grids from your collection

How to Play

Simply click the "Roll" button to generate a new color grid! The algorithm analyzes color distribution to determine rarity. Higher percentages of matching colors result in rarer tiers. Collect your favorites, chase the ultra-rare Scenic pieces, and build the ultimate collection!

Rarity System

  • Scenic Rare (1 in 7,000 per piece): Ultra-rare 4-piece collectibles
  • Secret Rare (1 in 4,000): Special pixel art designs
  • Perfect (100%): Pure single-color grids
  • Galaxy (95-99.9%): Highly uniform patterns
  • Mythic (90-94.9%): Rare coordinated colors
  • Elite (80-89.9%): Strong color dominance
  • Gold (70-79.9%): Good color distribution
  • Uncommon (60-69.9%): Moderate patterns
  • Common (Below 60%): Random distributions
