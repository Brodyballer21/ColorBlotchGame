At Roll Your Color ("we", "us", or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you sign in with Google Authentication, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your Google profile name
  • Your Google user ID (for authentication purposes)

1.2 Game Data

We store the following game-related data in Firebase Cloud Firestore:

  • Your color grid collection (saved rolls)
  • Your total roll count
  • Your Color Coin balance
  • Your best tier achieved
  • Your favorite grids
  • Your collected Scenic Rare pieces

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information including:

  • Browser type and version

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To save your progress and sync your collection across devices

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to:
